Bhakarwadi

Savor the taste of sweet, spice and all things nice with this Maharashtrian delicacy.

5,681Roti lovers worldwide 33New delighted customers

  • Preparation

    30 Mins

  • Cook

    5 Mins

  • Serves

    3-4 Pax

  • Reviews

    5 Reviews
Share the recipe with family and friends

Ingredients

1/2 tsp Lemon Juice
2 tsp Sesame Seeds
1 1/2 tsp Desiccated Coconut
1 tsp Poppy Seeds (Optional)
1/2 tsp Red Chilli Powder
1/2 tsp Coriander Powder
2 tsp Thin Sev
1/4 tsp Garam Masala
1/2 tsp Sugar

Rotimatic Setting

Recipe Puri
Flour Aashivaad Multigrain
Thickness 1
Roast 1
Oil 1

Flour Mix

1 Cup Aashirvaad Multigrain
1/2 Cup Maida

Steps

Step 1

Roast the sesame seeds, desiccated coconut and poppy seeds till light brown and fragrant.

Place mixture in a bowl and let it cool. Add the remaining ingredients to the bowl and mix well.

Step 2

Make puris using the Rotimatic settings.

Make sure that the puris are stored in a casserole until they are used, to prevent drying.

Step 3

Brush water on the puri, add the filling and press down a little. Make sure that the filling is moist. If it is not, add a few drops of water.

Roll the puri very tightly. Make sure the ends are sealed. Use water to seal them.

Tip: If you find that the water is not sealing the ends properly, make a slurry of refined flour and water and use that to seal the ends.
Step 4

Slice the rolled puris in 1/2 inch pieces. Fry till golden brown and Crisp. Enjoy!!

Tip: Freeze the rolled puris for 10 minutes to make slicing easier.
