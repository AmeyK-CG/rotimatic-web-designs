What it covers

Hardware
coverage

  • If you find any hardware defects, we’ll replace your machine for free.
  • There’s no restriction on the number of times your Rotimatic can be replaced.
  • Storage containers (flour, water and oil), kneading parts, cleaning kit, kicker, front door and power cord are not covered under warranty.

Remote
troubleshooting

  • Spotted a software issue? Our engineers will connect remotely to your Rotimatic to troubleshoot in a jiffy.
  • Enjoy priority tech support 24/7 via Live Chat including video call via the Rotimatic mobile app.
  • Speak to an expert via a call-back request.

Service at your
doorstep

  • Get all your questions answered and all your repairs done in the comfort of your home.
  • Enjoy free pickup and delivery with never an extra dollar for shipping, labor or any advanced support.

Repair and replacement in six simple steps

Step 1

Contact us via Live Chat on the Rotimatic mobile app or email us at support@zimplistic.com with your order number.

Step 2

We’ll verify if it’s a software failure (in which case, we’ll fix it remotely) or a hardware failure (in which case, we’ll replace your Rotimatic).

Step 3

We’ll send you a Return Label within 24-48 hours.

Step 4

Just print the label, pack your Rotimatic in the original box, and call the courier service as stated by us to arrange for pick up.

Step 5

We’ll send you a replacement unit via express delivery.

Step 6

You’ll receive your new Rotimatic within 7-10 days.
